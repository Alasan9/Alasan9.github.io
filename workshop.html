ABOUT THE WORKSHOP



The Workshop is a one week film production workshop curated by Silvia Gin,a student of Visual Anthropology at the Golsmith Universty in London.The workshop aim is to make a 20min film in collaboration with the people of international people and Refugee living in Amantea.
